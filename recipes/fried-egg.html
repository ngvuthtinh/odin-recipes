Fried Egg Recipe

Description

Heat oil or butter in a pan, crack an egg, and fry until the whites are set but the yolk remains runny. Serve hot with bread or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil or butter
  • A pinch of salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a pan on medium heat and add oil or butter.
  2. Crack the egg gently into the pan.
  3. Cook until the egg white is firm but the yolk is still runny. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.
