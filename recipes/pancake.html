Pancake Recipe

Description

Mix flour, milk, egg, and sugar into a smooth batter, then cook on a hot pan until golden brown on both sides. Serve warm with syrup or fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Butter or oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, milk, egg, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly greased pan on medium heat.
  3. Pour in some batter, cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side until golden. Serve warm.
