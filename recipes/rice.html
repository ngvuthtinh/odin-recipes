Rice Recipe

Description

Rinse 1 cup of rice, add 1½ cups of water, then cook in a rice cooker or pot until soft. Let it rest 10 minutes, fluff, and serve

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the rice 2–3 times until the water is clearer.
  2. Add the right amount of water (about 1.2–1.5 cups water per 1 cup rice) and start cooking.
  3. Let the rice rest 10 minutes after cooking, then fluff with a spoon and serve.
